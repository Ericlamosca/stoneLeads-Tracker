About StoneLeads


My name is Eric Johnson. I am from New Orleans, Louisana. StoneLeads was inspired by my day-to-day work as a veneer stone product salesman. I wanted to create a CRM that actually fits the rhythm of real sales — simple, intuitive, and built for people who value relationships as much as results. Version 1.0 us the foundation — a clean, functional prototype that will evolve into a full-featured system designed to help businesses track leads, manage customers, and grow with confidence


The Inspiration Behind StoneLeads
I built StoneLeads to solve the frustrations I saw firsthand — scattered spreadsheets, missed follow-ups, and lost opportunities. This project represents my vision for a smarter, more human approach to lead management.

I asked myself what I would want from a CRM — and then I built it"
